Your One Stop Solution To All Your Compounding Pharmacy Needs

We are your full-service Los Angeles based compounding pharmacy. Woodland Hills Pharmacy specializes in the formulation of drugs that are not commercially available. Our pharmacists use state-of-the-art technology so that your finished medication meets the doctor's exact specifications. Backed with advanced technology and a complete inventory of chemicals and formulas, your prescriptions will be prepared individually by an experienced Compounding Pharmacist.

Your Health Is Our Priority

Compounding is the art and science of preparing customized medications. Pharmacy began with pharmacists compounding a majority of the medications. We have embraced this lost art and built a compounding laboratory devoted to it. In our laboratory, we offer prescribers the ability to create a dosage that is specific to you. Many patients have difficulties with commercially available medications. Working with your doctor, we can offer you a custom-prepared medication in any dosage form.

Trust Us With Your Prescriptions

We guarantee great products because testing is an important step that we incorporate on a regular basis. Chemical testing means that we send samples out to an independent laboratory that tests our medications for potency and stability.
