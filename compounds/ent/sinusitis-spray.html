Sinusitis Nasal Spray

Mupirocin 0.2%, Itraconazole 1%, Xylitol 0.25%, Bismuth 0.025%, Triamcinolone 0.03% Nasal Spray

While oral antibiotics may be effective in fighting infections in the body, chronic sinusitis may respond well to the sinusitis nasal spray.

Bacteria have developed a way to survive against antibiotics. One way they are doing this is by creating biofilm. Biofilm consists of a layer of bacteria and mycotoxins that live together in a jelly like film. This film protects organisms from antibiotic and antifungal medications and makes them hard to kill.

The sinusitis nasal spray contains five key ingredients: The topical antibiotic mupirocin The topical antifungal itraconazole. The natural molecule xylitol, which kills bacteria and fights biofilm infections Bismuth to break up the biofilm Triamcinolone to shrink tissue swelling.

This is a prescription medication. We accept most major insurance plans.
