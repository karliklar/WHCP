Ear, Nose and Throat

Gentamicin 1% Solution

Gentamicin belongs to the class of antibiotics known as aminoglycosides. They have a broad spectrum of action, but are usually reserved for infections against gram-negative bacteria and staphylococcus colonies in the sinus cavity. Certain bacteria are found naturally on many people's skin and inside their nose. These bacteria are usually harmless and don't cause any problems. Gentamicin nasal spray is an effective way to eradicate, antibiotic resistant bacteria in the sinus cavity.

Nasal Spray Treatment

This method uses a nasal spray pump to deliver liquid gentamicin to the sinus area.

Atomized Treatment

This method uses an atomizer to pump in liquid gentamicin medication to the sinus area.

The compact electronic atomizer releases the liquid gentamicin medication into mist that gets inhaled into the sinus and nasal cavity regions.
