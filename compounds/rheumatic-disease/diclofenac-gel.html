Diclofenac Gel

ADiclofenac is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID). This medicine works by reducing substances in the body that cause pain and inflammation. Diclofenac is used to help with mild to moderate pain, or signs and symptoms of osteoarthritis or rheumatoid arthritis. Diclofenac Gel is a convenient and effective solution for a variety of pain-related conditions.

Fewer Risks and Side Effects Diclofenac gel is specifically designed to target the site of pain. Because diclofenac is applied topically, it does not circulate to the entire body. This means you have a powerful pain medication where you need it the most avoiding many of the complications common to oral medications.

Available in:
Diclofenac 10% Gel
Diclofenac 20% Gel
