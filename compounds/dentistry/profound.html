Lidocaine 10%, Prilocaine 10%, Tetracaine 4% Topical Anesthetic

aka Profound Gel, Tricaine Blue, DepBlu

Dental Gel

This gel is used as a pre-injection topical anesthetic for soft tissue and palatal procedures.

Often used before laser procedures, gingivoplasty and implant exposures, this gel is a combination of three active ingredients. It is often used as an alternative to local anesthetic injectable solutions.

This topical is available in five different flavors in an extra-thick gel and is packaged in plastic squeeze bottles (oral syringe and adapter included). We also offer this gel with phenylephrine 2% to prevent bleeding.

Dental Ointment

This topical is also available in a thick plasticized ointment base. This ointment can be applied with a Q-Tip or micro-brush to the dried mucosal surface prior to the procedure. It's a good choice for pre-injection numbing. We also offer this ointment with phenylephrine 2% to prevent bleeding.

Dental products are for office use and can only be ordered by a licensed dentist's office in the U.S. (some state law restrictions apply).
