Biotoxin Illness

Amphotericin B 0.25% Solution

Nasal Spray Treatment

Atomized intranasal amphotericin is the antifungal of choice to significantly reduce fungi and mycotoxins associated with biotoxin illness. Amphotericin b is a powerful antifungal that binds to the fungal cell membrane, thereby causing membrane leakage cell death.

Atomized intranasal EDTA is often used first to work against the biofilm coating clearing the way for a direct attack by the amphotericin. Once the biofilm is disrupted aerosolized intranasal amphotericin b is used to begin to clear the nasal colonized fungi and mycotoxins.
